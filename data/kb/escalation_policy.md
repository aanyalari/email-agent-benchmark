# Escalation Policy

Escalate angry customer messages that mention churn risk, renewal risk, legal risk, executive complaints, or repeated service failures.

Escalate VIP customer complaints to the account owner or support manager before promising credits, refunds, or contractual remedies.

Standard refund requests do not require escalation unless the customer reports a safety issue, legal concern, or urgent outage.

Escalation notes should include the customer tier, company, complaint summary, and requested outcome.
